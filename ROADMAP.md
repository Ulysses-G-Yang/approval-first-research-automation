# Roadmap

## v2.1.0 candidate: traceable research automation

- Product-first README in English and Chinese.
- Clear capability boundaries and offline example gallery.
- Local Page Evolution Lab for selector and page-state drift.
- `agent doctor` and workflow discovery for safer onboarding.
- Social preview asset, launch kit, issue templates, and expanded offline tests.

## Next: reviewed extensions, not unrestricted automation

- OCR integration for scanned PDF pages, with a visible provider and approval boundary.
- Reviewed `DraftPublisher` plugins that can save a platform draft after a separate sensitive approval.
- More local data transforms, document formats, and export targets.
- Reproducible extraction-compatibility fixtures contributed by the community.

## Intentionally out of scope

- Access-control bypass, anti-detection claims, or private-page harvesting.
- Arbitrary shell, Python, browser JavaScript, or model-installed plugins.
- Unreviewed automatic publication.
